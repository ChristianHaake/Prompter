# Privacy Policy

This app processes all your data locally in your browser.

- **No User Accounts:** You do not need to register.
- **No Uploads:** Your texts and projects are never uploaded to a server. They remain on your device.
- **Local Storage:** Your current draft is saved in your browser's local storage (`localStorage`) so you can resume your work later.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use analytics or tracking tools.

## Hosting
The app is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process connection data (like your IP address) to deliver the website securely.
